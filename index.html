  • Grief counselors on site. Re-entry requires card or ID + proof of residency.

Be Ready — Especially for Older Adults & Seniors

Go-bag (keep by the door)

  • Medications — 7+ days + written list
  • Glasses, hearing aids, mobility aids, CPAP
  • ID, insurance/Medicare cards, key documents
  • Phone chargers, cash, water, N95 masks
  • Pet carrier, leash, food & records

Plan now

  • Register for county emergency alerts
  • Agree on one out-of-area contact
  • Know two ways out (165, 96, 78, 67 often closed)
  • Keep gas tank above half; back car in
  • Check on neighbors who live alone
